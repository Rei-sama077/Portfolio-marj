Featured Projects

Whaloo

Whaloo is a mobile software that helps students increase their academic efficiency and stay organized. Whaloo, which features a joyful whale mascot, provides tools for managing study materials, tracking deadlines, creating to-do lists, and organizing class schedules. The software seeks to minimize stress and promote improved study habits by providing a user-friendly platform for students to stay on top of their academic duties. Whaloo helps users enhance their focus, productivity, and academic success by providing smart reminders and individualized study recommendations.
